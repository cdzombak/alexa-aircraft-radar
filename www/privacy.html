Aircraft Radar skill for Amazon Alexa

Privacy Policy

Aircraft Radar uses your device’s address to find your location and search for aircraft around you in response to your queries.

Your location information appears temporarily in a secured server log file which is used for troubleshooting when necessary. That log is cleared out regularly.

Your information is never stored permanently and is not used for any purpose other than searching for aircraft.

The Aircraft Radar skill is developed and run by Chris Dzombak.
